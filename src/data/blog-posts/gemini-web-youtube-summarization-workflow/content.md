## How to Maximize Your Summaries
The utility of this feature isn't just in the summary itself, but in the intelligent dialogue it enables.
Paste the Link and Prompt: Start with a simple command: "Summarize this article/video: [Paste Link]."
Request Specific Formats: Don't settle for a generic paragraph. Ask Gemini to:
- "Give me a bulleted list of the key points."
- "Identify the pros and cons discussed in this video."
- "Explain the core concept in layman's terms."
Cross-Reference and Research: Use the summary as a jumping-off point for further inquiry. "Based on this summary, what is the latest update on [related topic]?"
